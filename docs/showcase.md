# Why CleanText Studio?

AI-generated and copied text is often useful but poorly packaged. It can carry excessive headings, presentation-only Markdown, strange spacing, repeated labels, broken tables, and formulas that do not survive a normal copy-and-paste workflow.

CleanText Studio restores a professional document without presenting itself as a tool for bypassing AI or plagiarism detection. Its purpose is practical editorial cleanup: retain useful content and structure, review the result, and export a document that can be edited and shared.

## A structure-first approach

Instead of flattening everything into plain text, the application keeps headings, lists, quotations, tables, code, links, and recognized formula runs as document blocks. That shared structure feeds both Preview mode and DOCX export.

## Local-first by default

Basic cleanup, preview, and export stay on the device. Optional AI refinement is a deliberate BYOK action: the user chooses the provider, model, endpoint, and key. This distinction makes the data flow understandable before text leaves the machine.

## Professional delivery

The exporter writes structured DOCX documents, including native Word tables and supported editable OMML equations. TXT remains available when a simple UTF-8 handoff is the better fit.

## Who it helps

- Writers and researchers cleaning copied notes before editing.
- Knowledge workers turning web extracts into shareable documents.
- Teams that need a local-first step before a controlled optional AI workflow.
- Developers and translators who want a desktop open-source project with a focused, testable scope.
