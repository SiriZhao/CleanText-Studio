# Indie Hackers post draft

## Title

Building a local-first document cleanup tool for the messy handoff after AI-assisted writing

## Post

CleanText Studio began with a repetitive problem: copied material and drafts
were useful, but their formatting had to be rebuilt before they could become a
working Word document. The project focuses on structure recovery, not content
generation: headings, lists, tables, links, and supported equations should
remain editable after cleanup.

The desktop app is open source and local-first for normal cleanup/export. AI
refinement is optional and user-configured. I am looking for feedback on
practical document workflows, reproducible DOCX edge cases, and contribution
experience.
